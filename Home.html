Welcome to AutoLanka

Autolanka.com is the first automobile e-magazine in Sri Lanka which focused towards Sri-Lankan motoring market place.

It provides State of the Art services to thousands of clients accessing the site every day. By now, it has become so popular that the name "AutoLanka.com” has become a household name among motor enthusiasts and car buyers/sellers in Sri Lanka.At AutoLanka.com we aim to provide a streamlined car buying and selling process and allow our customers to be confident when it comes to trading vehicles through us..
